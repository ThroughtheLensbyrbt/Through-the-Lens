About Me

Hey! I am Roggen Trinidad but you can call me “Gen” I am the person behind Through the Lens and I am 22 years old, living from San Pedro Laguna and currently studying Bachelor of Multi-Media Arts at STI Santa Rosa Laguna, I have 3 years of experience in the field of Photography and now taking it to the next level to be able to grow as a Professional Photographer. I only started this journey just a year ago by having to shoot at our local church and I grew to love this field of art and wanted to express more and by September I started talking to other creatives who are free to shoot with and do portraits. Mostly of my works are Portraits, that is where I'm seeing myself excel. I also love to do concerts, the feelings and emotions of the musicians and also of the audience is what I like to capture in that kind of shoot. I am not skilled in terms of drawing but I want to share through photographs, this is the way that I can express my art.
